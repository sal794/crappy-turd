## Skin Powers

Existing powers for reference:
- **The Dook** — one extra life
- **Craptain America** — shield (extra life); regained after passing 25 gates

### Power Ideas

- **Gap Expander** — obstacles have slightly wider gaps for this skin
- **Breather** — every 15 gates, the next gap is guaranteed extra wide
- **Hold to Fly** — instead of tapping to flap, player holds the button/screen to ascend continuously and releases to descend. Completely changes the feel of the game. Would need a per-skin input mode toggle in `PlayerController`.
- **Double Flap** — can flap twice before gravity pulls down again
- **Shrink** — hitbox is smaller than the sprite appears
- **Slow-Mo** — game briefly slows down after each gate passed
- **Freeze** — once per run, freeze all obstacles for 2 seconds
- **Score Multiplier** — every gate counts as 2 points for a stretch
- **Comeback Kid** — after a near-miss, next gate is worth double
- **Ghost Mode** — phase through one obstacle per run, then recharges
